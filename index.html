My Story

When I was a child, I loved video games and had a passion for technology. I always wanted to be the person to solve complex problems and especially ones related to technology

I went to UCCS to study Physics and Mathematics, but I also took classes in C# and C++ while I was there. I even took game design as an elective. I chose to major in physics because I loved problem solving, and I exceeded in both math and physics!

While studying, I built several C++ applications for an escape room I was working at. I only worked with those projects for a short preiod of time, but from then on I knew I wanted to develop software for my career. To me, it feels so good to spend time solving problems and getting paid for it! It is exactly the type of thing I could enjoy doing for a very long time. There is nothing like the thrill of the intellectual stimulation of these problems to a person like me!

After researching for a while, I decided to start learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript inside and out. I have now been mastering this art for over 6 months and have 20+ projects to show for it. At this point, I love learning this stuff so much that I can't imagine a lifestyle without coding in it!

To maintain my skills, I continue to practice learning algorithms and data structures using sites like HackerRank, where I also occasionally work on some of their competitions. I also am continuously making websites and web applications for me and my friends!
